Our Story

It all started in a small kitchen filled with the aroma of warm vanilla and freshly baked bread. Sweet Treats Bakery was born from a lifelong passion for baking, passed down through generations.

In 2020, our founder, Emma Collins, decided to turn her grandmother's cherished recipes into a business that could bring joy to others. What began as a small home-based bakery with a team of 4 people bakery quickly became a local favorite, known for its handcrafted cakes, soft pastries, and artisan bread made with love and the finest organic ingredients.

Our Mission & Values

  • Quality Ingredients: We use only the finest organic and locally sourced ingredients to ensure freshness and flavor in every bite.
  • Family & Tradition: Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the heart and soul of homemade baking.
  • Community First: We believe in giving back to our community and creating a warm, welcoming space for everyone.
  • Passion for Baking: Every cake, pastry, and loaf of bread is made with love and care, because we know that food brings people together.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to eco-friendly packaging and reducing food waste wherever possible.
